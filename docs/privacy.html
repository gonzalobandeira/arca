Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. Period.

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

At Arcav, we believe that your financial security data is highly sensitive and should remain private. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy.

1. Data Collection

Arcav does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or coordinate card information to external servers.

All data you scan or enter into the app is stored locally on your device within its secure enclave. We do not have access to your cards, your biometric data, or your usage patterns.

2. Biometric Authentication

Arcav uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework (Face ID/Touch ID). The app never receives your actual biometric data; it only receives a success or failure notification from the iOS operating system.

3. Third-Party Services

Arcav does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

4. Security

Data stored within the app is encrypted using industry-standard methods provided by the iOS platform. We recommend using a strong device passcode and keeping your iOS software up to date.
